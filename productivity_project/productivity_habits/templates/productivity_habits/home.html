6 Key Productivity Habits

Implement these habits to transform your productivity and well-being

Quarterly Quests

Instead of annual goals, set 3-4 meaningful objectives for the next 90 days. These are easier to visualize and accomplish.

Weekly Reset

Spend 20 minutes each week reviewing your progress, celebrating wins, and setting priorities for the week ahead.

Morning Manifesto

A 2-minute morning check-in to review your weekly priorities and set your most important task for the day.

Focus Log

Track how many minutes you spend in focused work. Simply measuring your focus time will help improve it.

Standing Order Social Events

Schedule regular social events that happen on a fixed schedule without needing to coordinate each time.

Multimodality Multitasking

Make productive use of "dead time" by combining tasks that use different modalities (e.g., walking while listening).
